A / Why purchase with ChromaHive?
ChromaHive is a website for selling art that puts sellers and buyers into contact, whether they are amateurs, collectors, professionals or artists. We carefully review all of the art advertisements before they are published. The following criteria are reviewed: the artist, technique/medium, size of the piece, state of conservation, pedigree/provenance, certificate of authenticity, etc. We will help you search for the perfect piece while keeping your desired subject matter and budget in mind. We will offer you a wide range of quality works. We also offer an Art Advisory service to assist and advise you during the purchasing process. This service is completely free, simply contact us at the following address: contact@ChromaHive.com or fill out this questionnaire so that we can better understand your taste in art and expectations.

B / Why create an account?
You must create an account, by clicking here, if you wish to enjoy our many services -newsletter, list of favorites, follow your favorite artists, communicate within the community, create alerts, get personalized assistance.

C / How is the price of a piece of art determined?
The seller decides upon the price. However, if the price does not reflect the actual market value, we will ask the seller to change their price. If they refuse, the piece will be removed from our website.

D / How is the authenticity and quality of the art verified?
We carefully curate the art available on our website in order to guarantee their quality and authenticity. Our sales request form asks for as much information as possible, including photographs that show the entire piece and details as well as the certificate of authenticity. If there is any doubt about a piece we will not put it up for sale on the website.

E / Can I negotiate the posted price?
If the seller has selected the "price negotiation" option, you can try and negotiate the price of the artwork by clicking on the "make an offer" button. Your offer will be sent to the seller immediately and they can accept or refuse it, in the latter case, they can make a counter-offer.

A / What are the accepted payment methods?
Credit card payments: Visa, MasterCard, American Express and bank transfers. In order to reduce the chance of fraud, all payments are subject to a 3D Secure validation. For more information, click here (T&C).

B / Can I have an invoice for my order?
You can find and download your invoice in your personal account. Please note that the billing address may not be the same as the delivery address.
